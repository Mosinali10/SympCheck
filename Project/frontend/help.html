FAQ & Resources

Understanding how to use the AI assessment tool effectively and safely.

How accurate is the AI assessment?
SympCheck leverages late-stage large language models calibrated against clinical datasets. While highly effective at identifying triage levels, it is not a diagnostic tool. Always consult a physician for official medical decisions.
What should I do in an emergency?
If our AI flags your situation as "EMERGENCY," you should bypass digital tools and immediately contact your local emergency response service (911/112).
Is my data HIPAA compliant?
While this is a public informational tool, we apply HIPAA-grade privacy principles, including end-to-end encryption and zero-retention policies for identifiable health data.
