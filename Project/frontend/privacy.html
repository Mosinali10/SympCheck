Trust & Transparency

Privacy Policy

How we protect your personal health data through encryption and strict data minimization protocols.

Data Encryption

All symptom assessments are encrypted in transit using industry-standard TLS 1.3. We do not store identifiable health records longer than necessary to provide the triage results.

Anonymized Analysis

When you interact with our AI, your symptoms are processed anoymously. We do not link your genetic or identifiable personal data to clinical queries.

Third-Party Policy

We do not sell data to pharmaceutical or insurance entities. Our partnership with Google Gemini is strictly for computational reasoning purposes.
